The Builder's Dictionary

Before we launch, let's decode the technical jargon into plain English. Here is how EVE Frontier technology works under the hood.

What is a Smart Assembly?

The building blocks of civilization. They are customizable structures that host player-made mods within the galaxy, allowing you to shape the Frontier in real-time.

From smart gates and storage units to automated turrets, they put unprecedented freedom into your hands to create a massively moddable universe.

OwnerCap & Energy

Programmable in-game structures physically anchor in space and connect to NetworkNodes for energy to remain online.

Every assembly comes with an OwnerCap. Whoever holds this digital key controls the structure, meaning you can authorize custom rules or safely transfer ownership to other players.

The Sui Blockchain

The ultra-fast digital rulebook powering the economy. EVE Frontier uses zkLogin (login with email/socials) and Sponsored Transactions (gas is paid for you).

Thanks to Sui's object-centric model, every single ship, item, and Smart Assembly is a unique, persistent digital asset with an unbreakable history—no crypto wallets required.

Move & Witness Types

The highly secure coding language used to write EVE Smart Contracts.

To extend an assembly, the AI writes a Move package using a Witness Type—a cryptographic key ensuring only your custom logic can interact with your structure. You don't need to learn to code. The AI handles the language for you.

The AI Interactive Roadmap

Follow these 5 steps to deploy your first custom structure. Click through the tabs below to progress.

1

Prepare Your Toolkit

Before giving commands, assemble your command center. You only need two things:

  • EVE Frontier Game Client: Installed and logged into the Utopia server (the developer sandbox environment).
  • An AI Assistant: We highly recommend Cursor (an AI code editor) or a powerful chatbot like ChatGPT (GPT-4) or Claude.
2

Initialize the AI Architect

AI models are smart, but they need the EVE manuals. Paste this "Master Prompt" into your AI to give it total context.

I want to build a Smart Assembly in the game EVE Frontier, which uses the Sui blockchain and the Move programming language. I have zero programming experience, so I need you to act as my expert developer and guide me step-by-step. First, please review these official resources to understand how the game and the code work: 1. EVE Frontier Build Hub: https://evefrontier.com/en/build 2. EVE Frontier Docs: https://docs.evefrontier.com/ 3. Move on EVE Frontier: https://evefrontier.space/move/ Once you understand the documentation, please explain to me in simple terms: How do I set up my computer (Mac/Windows) to start building? What tools do I need to download (like the Sui CLI or wallet), and how do I configure them to connect to the Utopia test server?

3

Follow the Blueprint

After receiving your prompt, the AI will provide a simple, numbered list of setup tasks tailored to your operating system.

Pro-Tip for Beginners

If the AI uses a term you don't know, just ask! Say: "I didn't understand step 2 regarding the CLI, can you explain it like I'm five years old and tell me exactly what buttons to click?" The AI has infinite patience.

4

Architect Your Vision

With your environment ready, it's time to brainstorm. Talk to your AI like a creative partner and describe your ideal Smart Assembly.

YOU

"I want to create a Smart Assembly. It should be a public storage locker in space. Anyone can put items in, but if they want to take an item out, they have to pay 10 in-game tokens to my wallet. Can you write the Move code for this?"

"Absolutely! Let's build that. Here is the Move smart contract code for a toll-based storage locker, along with instructions on where to save this file..."

5

Deploy & Launch

The AI will generate the actual code and give you the exact terminal commands required to push your new Smart Assembly onto the Sui blockchain.

Simply copy the code, paste it where the AI tells you, and run the deployment commands. Within minutes, your custom creation will manifest inside the EVE Frontier universe!
